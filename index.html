A first year graduate student in Materials Science and Engineering at Stanford University

I'm really passionate about science outreach and communication.

I am a future graduate student in Materials Science and Engineering at Stanford University (starting in Fall 2020) who is passionate about advancing the frontiers of scientific research. Throughout my time in undergrad at NC State University, I've conducted research at Caltech, Eastman Chemical Company, and on campus. These projects involved materials characterization via electron microscopy, electrochemistry, catalysis, and learning how to problem-solve in a research lab.

In addition to research, I am also passionate about science communication. With an enthusiasm that's often described as infectious, I enjoy getting people of all ages excited about science through demonstrations at K-12 schools, colleges, museums, and public libraries.
